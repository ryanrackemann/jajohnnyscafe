Welcome to J.A. Johnny's Cafe

A note from the owner

Thank you for visiting J.A. Johnny's Cafe.

We are a very small neighborhood cafe that takes pride in fresh and tasty ingredients. Our goal is to serve you the freshest and best items on the Parker Strip.

Please keep in mind that this little place can draw a pretty awesome crowd at times. We aim to provide great food and fun for the entire family, and we do our best to do so in the timeliest manner possible.

At the busiest of times, this can run on "River Time," so please sit back and relax and enjoy yourselves. You deserve it!
